What is the motivation for creating an Internet of Things? The motivation is to make life easier by connecting devices and sharing data automatically. How do we benefit from this increased interconnectivity? We benefit by saving time, improving efficiency, and controlling devices remotely. What are the risks associated with this kind of connectivity? The risks include hacking, loss of privacy, and data being stolen. How does cybersecurity relate to the Internet of Things? Cybersecurity is important because connected devices can be attacked, so they must be protected. What devices do you use and why is it important to protect them? I use a phone and a computer. It is important to protect them to keep personal information safe and prevent hacking.
