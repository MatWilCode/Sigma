--- ## **Computer Scientist Name** Raymond Tomlinson --- ## **Education** Raymond Tomlinson studied at **Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute** and **MIT**. He earned a **Bachelor’s degree** and a **Master’s degree**. His highest education was at **MIT**. --- ## **4 Fun Facts** 1. He invented email. 2. He used the “@” symbol for email addresses. 3. The first email was only a test message. 4. He did not know email would become very important. --- ## **Details About Their Life** Raymond Tomlinson was an American computer scientist. He worked at a company called BBN. He helped create a way to send messages between computers. This work was part of the early internet. His invention of email changed how people communicate all over the world. --- ## **Field of Study** * Computer Science * Computer Networking --- ## **Contributions to Computer Science** Raymond Tomlinson is best known for **inventing email**. He also introduced the **“@” symbol** in email addresses. --- ## **References (NO WIKI)** * [https://www.computerhistory.org/profile/raymond-tomlinson/](https://www.computerhistory.org/profile/raymond-tomlinson/) * [https://www.internethalloffame.org/inductee/raymond-tomlinson/](https://www.internethalloffame.org/inductee/raymond-tomlinson/)
