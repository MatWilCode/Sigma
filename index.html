Go to the **Macaws**. Return to the **Front Entrance**. --- ## 3. Route Where You Never Turn Right (5 pts) Start at the **Front Entrance**. Go straight to the **Fish** exhibit. Follow the path back to the **Front Entrance**. --- ## 4. Route With a Food Stop (5 pts) Start at the **Front Entrance**. Go to the **Fish** exhibit. Walk to the **Swans**.
