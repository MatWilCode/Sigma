Siap, ini aku buatin **space blog POV (jadi Gherman Titov)** pakai bahasa Inggris sederhana (kayak Google Translate, level SMP) 👍 --- **My Space Journey – By Gherman Titov** **August 5, 1961 – Before Launch** Today I feel excited but also a little nervous. I trained very hard for this mission. I checked my equipment and listened carefully to instructions. I know this mission is very important for my country and for space exploration. **August 6, 1961 – Launch Day** The rocket started to shake and move very fast. I felt strong G-forces pushing my body into the seat. It was hard to move, but I stayed calm. After some time, I finally reached space. **In Space – Vostok 2 Mission** When I was in space, I felt weightlessness for the first time. My body was floating, and it felt strange. At one moment, I felt dizzy and sick. Later, people called this space sickness. I looked at Earth from space, and it was very beautiful. I also took photos and observed how my body reacted in space. I stayed in space for about 25 hours and orbited Earth many times. **August 7, 1961 – Return to Earth** When I came back to Earth, I felt very tired but happy. The landing was not easy, but I was safe. I was proud because I showed that humans can live in space for a long time. This mission is one of the most important moments in my life. I will never forget my journey to space.
